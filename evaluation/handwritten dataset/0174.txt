Magical Realism is a genre of literature
which represents the real  world as having
an influence of magic and #. fantasy .
In this work , the world it represents is a
real world but fantastical elements are
considered normal in this world . Here are
some of the characteristics of magical
realism :
All magical realism novels take place in a
setting in this world that's familiar to the
reader .
From talking objects to dead characters to 
ideas , every magical elements are fantastical 
that do not #occur in this world . However , 
they are presented as normal in the novel . 
The author of magical realism intentionally
leave the magic unexplained and reinforce
that it is part of everyday life .
Authors often use magical realism to express critique
of society .
