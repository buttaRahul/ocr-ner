Noun :
The name of places , person or
things is called Noun .
For example Peshawar , # Islamabad , Quaid-e-azam 
Chairs , Orange , etc .
Kind of Noun .
(1) Countable Noun :- 
Those thing which can # be countied 
is called countable Noun e.g Apples , students etc
(2) Noun-countable Noun :- 
Those things which can not be counted 
is called non-countable noun .
