Post-modernism is the age around 20th
century , in which we particularly
study about the texts , works which
is written after World war 2 and 
the writers of that age are particularly 
known as post-modernists . 
Some of the features or characteristics 
of this era are ; Magical Realism , 
Inter-texuality and science development
or Science destruction etc . 
Magical Realism is about the 
things which are seem to be real
but they are not . Such as in the 
novel " One hundred years of Solitude " 
we see that most of the things
or events are explained or shown 
as that they are real but in 
reality that totally based on fiction 
and fantastical things / elements .
